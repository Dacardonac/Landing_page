Info About Pekka

The Pekka. It is a powerful troop and can do quite a bit of damage with the right placement and support. Deals massive damage to one target at a time.

Can a Pekka kill a Barbarian in one hit. This is useful to keep in mind if your opponent's only counterattack against your Pekka is Barbarians. Troops that deal area damage, like the Wizard, can help you clear the path of swarms, but a quick spell can get rid of him, taking into account his low hit points and making it easier to defeat your Pekka.
